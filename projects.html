Our Projects

Our projects page is a curated collection of timeless classics, each meticulously restored to its former glory.

From the iconic 1964 Alfa Romeo Giulia Sprint Speciale, with its race-ready heritage, to the rugged 1973 Land Rover Series 3, embodying off-road prowess, and the elegant 1972 Porsche 911, a symbol of performance and design — each project is a masterpiece, and each car tells a unique story.

Immerse yourself in the passion, craftsmanship, and dedication that define our work, where every vehicle is a chapter in the rich history of automotive excellence.

1964 Alfa Romeo Giulia Sprint Speciale

The blue 1964 Alfa Romeo Giulia Sprint Speciale, restored by Eternal Garage, is a classic car icon. Designed by Franco Scaglione of Bertone, it was a race-ready version of the Giulietta, powered by a sophisticated Alfa Romeo DOHC inline 4-cylinder engine. Its design drew inspiration from the sleek Carrozzeria Touring “Disco Volante” racing cars and Bertone’s futuristic Berlina Aerodinamica Technica (BAT) experimental show cars.

Eternal Garage’s restoration project preserved these unique features while enhancing performance and comfort. The team restored the car’s bodywork and overhauled the original engine, ensuring it runs as smoothly and powerfully as it did in 1964.

The interior was restored to its original glory, with premium leather seats and meticulously refinished dashboard and trim details. The result is a beautifully restored blue 1964 Alfa Romeo Giulia Sprint Speciale that offers a driving experience that rivals contemporary models.

1973 Land Rover Series 3

Eternal Garage undertook the restoration of a black 1973 Land Rover Series 3, a vehicle renowned for its durability and off-road prowess. The exterior was meticulously restored, preserving the iconic silhouette and original black paint. Any signs of wear were repaired, resulting in a vehicle that mirrors its original 1973 condition.

Under the hood, the original engine was overhauled. Each component was checked and restored or replaced as necessary, ensuring the vehicle runs as smoothly and reliably as it did in its prime.

The interior was also restored, with seats reupholstered in premium leather and the dashboard and trim details refinished. The result is a comfortable interior that stays true to the vehicle’s utilitarian roots. The final product is a beautifully restored black 1973 Land Rover Series 3, ready to take on any terrain just like it did decades ago.

1972 Porsche 911

The team embarked on a restoration project of a 1972 Porsche 911, a car renowned for its performance and design. This particular model, with its pale yellow paint job, is a testament to the vibrant style of the 70s.

The exterior restoration preserved the car’s iconic silhouette and the unique pale yellow paint, enhancing its vintage charm. The original 2.4-litre flat-six engine, known for its power and reliability, was meticulously overhauled. Each component was checked and restored or replaced as necessary, ensuring the car runs as smoothly and powerfully as it did in its prime.

Inside, the team restored the car’s interior, reupholstering the seats in premium leather and refinishing the dashboard and trim details. The result is a beautifully restored 1972 Porsche 911 that not only pays homage to its rich history but also offers a driving experience that rivals contemporary models. This project stands as a testament to the team’s passion for classic cars and their commitment to preserving their beauty and performance for future generations to appreciate.

Each project a masterpiece, each car a story.
