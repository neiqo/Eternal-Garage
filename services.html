Our Services

Embark on a journey into the realm of bespoke automotive services, with a focus on Events, Transport, and Training. Whether you seek exclusive gatherings, secure transportation, or hands-on training experiences, our passion for automotive heritage ensures a unique and unforgettable adventure. Rely on our expertise to provide exceptional services, turning your classic car encounters into a celebration of timeless elegance.

Events
Over the past decade, Eternal Garage has been a sought-after partner for film, photography, music, and fashion projects. Our classic cars starred in iconic movies like Once Upon a Time in Hollywood and Midnight In Paris. Renowned artists like Adele chose our vehicles, and brands such as BMW and Nike featured them in ads. Our presence at events like the Cannes Film Festival since 2016 reflects our commitment to enhancing special occasions. At Eternal Garage, we believe classic cars add nostalgia and sophistication to any event. Our collection, available for hire with a driver, has graced numerous weddings and major events, creating unforgettable experiences.
Transport
We take pride in ensuring the secure and seamless transportation of your cherished vehicles. Whether relocating for an event, exhibition, or personal reasons, our dedicated team guarantees a safe journey for your classic automobiles. With a profound understanding of the unique needs of vintage cars, we provide a transportation experience that aligns with the same level of care and excellence that defines your classic automotive passion. Trust us to deliver your prized possessions to their destination with the utmost precision and reliability.
Training
Designed for enthusiasts and beginners alike, our workshops provide a hands-on exploration of car maintenance, restoration, and advanced driving techniques. Led by seasoned instructors, our courses offer more than just skill acquisition—they provide a gateway to a community of like-minded individuals passionate about the timeless allure of classic cars. Immerse yourself in an educational journey that goes beyond the basics, elevating your understanding and proficiency in the world of automobiles. Join us and become a part of a community that shares your love for classic cars and the art of driving

Crafting unforgettable moments with our bespoke services.
