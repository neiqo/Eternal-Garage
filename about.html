About Us

Established in 2015, Eternal Garage is not just a workshop; it's a tribute to the enduring charm of vintage automobiles. Founded by dedicated car enthusiasts, our mission is to celebrate and safeguard classic car culture. From meticulous restorations to bespoke services, we honor the artistry of the past with unwavering commitment and passion. Welcome to Eternal Garage, where every detail tells a story of timeless elegance.

2015

Eternal Garage was founded by passionate automotive enthusiasts with a vision to celebrate and preserve classic car culture. The workshop opened its doors, specializing in meticulous restorations.

2016

Eternal Garage hosted its inaugural classic car show, attracting enthusiasts and collectors from far and wide. The event showcased the team's dedication to craftsmanship and attention to detail.

2017

Building on its success, Eternal Garage experienced continuous growth, establishing itself as a reputable workshop for vintage vehicle restoration.

2018

Recognizing a demand for bespoke automotive experiences, Eternal Garage expanded its services to include exclusive events, secure transportation, and personalized training programs.

2019

The workshop actively engaged with the classic car community, organizing charity events and supporting initiatives aimed at preserving automotive heritage.

2020

Eternal Garage faced the unique challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic. The workshop swiftly adapted, implementing rigorous safety protocols. While physical interactions were limited, the team remained committed to providing top-notch service, utilizing the time to enhance online offerings and foster virtual connections with vintage car enthusiasts.

2021

Eternal Garage collaborated with renowned automotive designers to create limited-edition vehicles, blending classic aesthetics with modern engineering, marking a new era for the company.

2022

The workshop gained international recognition, featured in prestigious automotive publications and winning awards for excellence in classic car restoration and innovation.

2023

Eternal Garage launched educational programs, sharing its expertise with aspiring craftsmen and classic car enthusiasts. The company continued to be a beacon for timeless automotive elegance.
